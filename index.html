BRISTOL

The East Bay Bike Path in Bristol offers stunning coastal views, connecting parks like Independence Park with Colt State Park, featuring birding opportunities, especially near the Audubon Nature Center stop where you can walk a boardwalk for bay vistas, making it a family-friendly, flat, paved trail for biking, walking, and enjoying seaside New England charm, despite occasional crowding and seasonal marsh smells.
